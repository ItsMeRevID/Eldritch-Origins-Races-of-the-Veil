## About

**Eldritch Origins: Races of the Veil** is an immersive fantasy modpack for Minecraft 1.16.5 centered around the **Fantasia RPG Races** mod. At the very start of your journey, you are presented with a race selection screen that fundamentally shapes your gameplay, abilities, and story within the world of Aetherion.

## Lore

In the ancient era, the world of **Aetherion** was protected by the **Veil** — a luminous barrier separating the mortal realm from the infinite Eldritch Beyond. 

The **Fracturing** shattered this Veil, infusing the land with raw arcane power and awakening diverse bloodlines. You awaken as one of the **Veil-touched**. The remnants of the Veil offer you a single, fateful choice: **Which bloodline will you claim?**

Your race is not cosmetic — it is your origin, your power, and your destiny.

## Features

- **12 Playable Fantasy Races** from the Fantasia RPG Races mod (each with 3 buffs and 2 debuffs)
- Deep RPG elements with race-influenced progression and world interactions
- Rich high-fantasy atmosphere with enhanced exploration and magic
- Carefully balanced survival gameplay
- Beautiful visuals and immersive sound design
- Performance-friendly mod selection

## Installation

1. Download the latest version from [CurseForge](#)
2. Install using your preferred launcher (CurseForge App, Prism Launcher, etc.)
3. Launch the game and select your race upon entering a new world

**Minecraft Version**: 1.16.5 (Forge)

## Recommended Settings

- **RAM Allocation**: 6–8 GB minimum (8+ GB recommended)
- **Render Distance**: 12–16 chunks
- **Shaders** (Optional): Compatible with most fantasy shaders
